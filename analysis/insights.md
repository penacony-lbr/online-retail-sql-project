# Retail Business Insights

This document summarizes key business insights extracted from the Online Retail II dataset using SQL and BigQuery.

---

##  1. Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue is stable between **500K–700K per month**.
- Clear seasonal peak from **March to June**.
- Customer activity remains steady (**900–1200 active customers/month**).
- Business exhibits strong recurring demand.

---

##  2. Top Products
- Best-selling items are mostly **giftware & decorative products**.
- Top SKU contributes a significant portion of order volume.
- High-volume items are not always the highest-revenue items → opportunity for pricing strategy.

---

##  3. Top Customers (VIP)
- A small number of customers contribute disproportionately to total revenue.
- Top 10 customers = **8–12% of total revenue** → classic Pareto pattern.
- High-value customers show high purchase frequency and larger basket sizes.

---

##  4. Revenue by Country
- United Kingdom dominates (majority of transactions).
- Top international markets include **Netherlands, EIRE, Germany**.
- International customers tend to place larger orders but less frequently.

---

##  5. RFM Segmentation
- RFM analysis identifies a strong high-value segment (R=5, F=5, M=5).
- Many customers show high recency but low frequency → opportunity for re-engagement campaigns.
- Monetary value is heavily skewed toward top spenders.

---

##  6. Cohort Retention
- Strong retention in the first **2–3 months** after acquisition.
- Retention gradually decreases over time (common in retail).
- 2011 cohorts show slightly improved retention performance.

---

##  Business Recommendations
- Expand marketing efforts around seasonal peaks (Mar–Jun).
- Increase promotions for high-margin items, not just high-volume ones.
- Explore loyalty program for top customers.
- Improve international checkout/shipping to grow outside UK.
- Re-target customers with high recency but low frequency.
